How to Play

  • Each day, a new mystery country is selected.
  • Everyone gets the same country, it rotates at midnight UK time.
  • Your goal is to guess all of its **land borders** (neighboring countries).
  • Enter a country name from the dropdown. If it's a valid border, it will turn green on the map.
  • You get 5 chances, guess wisely!
  • In Hard Mode, the main country's name is hidden until you guess it.
  • When all borders are guessed or you've used all attempts, the game ends.
